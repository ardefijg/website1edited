2. Gravon.net

Gravon.net is home to a number of online board games and card games, including Tichu, Balloon Cup, and Stratego Original. Much of the site is in English, although some of the text is in German. It is presented by Stratego Deutschland e.V. You can choose to play with European or American pieces.
