Pieces

Each player has 40 pieces representing an army, which consists of 33 moveable and 7 non-moveable pieces. Moveable pieces, ranked from high to low, are:

  • (10) Marshal: 1 piece
  • (9) General: 1 piece
  • (8) Colonel: 2 pieces
  • (7) Major: 3 pieces
  • (6) Captain: 4 pieces
  • (5) Lieutenant: 4 pieces
  • (4) Sergeant: 4 pieces
  • (3) Miner: 5 pieces
  • (2) Scout: 8 pieces
  • (1/S) Spy: 1 pieces

Non-moveable (unranked) pieces are:

  • (B) Bomb: 6 pieces
  • (F) Flag: 1 piece
